# Data Privacy Notice

**Source:** AdZU College Student Handbook, Appendix B
**Effective:** School Year 2018–2019

## Chapter I: Introduction

Welcome to Ateneo de Zamboanga University. As an educational institution, AdZU is committed to ensuring that data privacy is protected. This privacy notice tells you how the University collects, uses, discloses, and transfers data obtained from you. If you are the parent or guardian of an applicant or student who is a minor, this policy refers to the personal data of the applicant or your child/ward.

In compliance with the **Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA)** and the implementing rules and regulations set by the **National Privacy Commission (NPC)**, the University ensures that the data collected is protected while maintaining the free flow of information necessary for operations.

The data collected includes information classified as **personal information, sensitive personal information, and privileged information** as stipulated in the DPA — information that identifies you, protected by law, and handled according to the policies stated herein.

## Chapter II: When and How We Create, Collect, Acquire, or Generate Information

Personal information is collected, acquired, or generated in various forms — written or electronic records, photographic and video images, and biometric information — through:

a. **Admission.** When you apply for admission, we collect:
   - Name, email address, telephone number, and other contact details
   - Personal circumstances data: family history, previous school attended, academic performance, disciplinary record, employment history, medical records

b. **Enrollment and Period of Residency.** After enrollment, we collect:
   - **Academic/curricular data** — classes enrolled, scholastic performance, attendance record, etc.
   - **Co-curricular engagements** — service learning, outreach, internship/apprenticeship
   - **Extra-curricular activities** — student organization membership, leadership positions, participation in seminars, competitions, programs, outreach, study tours
   - **Disciplinary cases** — including sanctions imposed

c. **Other data acquired during stay:**
   - Pictures or videos from official documentation
   - Recordings from CCTV cameras installed on school premises

d. **Unsolicited Information.** Information received without prior consent. If not relevant to educational purposes, it is discarded. If pertinent to legitimate educational interests, it is treated with utmost protection per the DPA.

## Chapter III: How We Use Your Information

Data is processed (to the extent permitted by law) for academic, administrative, research, historical, and statistical purposes, specifically:

a. Evaluate applications for admission
b. Process enrollment of old, new, and transfer students
c. Record, generate, and maintain records of academic, co-curricular, and extracurricular progress
d. Record, store, and evaluate student work (homework, seatwork, quizzes, long tests, exams, term papers, theses, research papers, reflection papers, essays, presentations)
e. Record, generate, and maintain records of class attendance and participation
f. Establish and maintain student information systems
g. Share grades between and among faculty members and others with official need for academic deliberations
h. Process scholarship applications, grants, and financial assistance
i. Investigate incidents relating to student behavior for disciplinary measures
j. Maintain directories and alumni records
k. Provide services — health, insurance, counseling, IT, library, sports/recreation, transportation, parking, campus mobility, safety, security
l. Manage and control access to campus facilities and equipment
m. Communicate official school announcements
n. Share marketing and promotional materials regarding school-related functions, events, projects, activities
o. Compile and generate reports for statistical and research purposes
p. Solicit participation in research and non-commercial surveys
q. Share information with persons/institutions as stated in Chapter IV

The DPA imposes **stricter rules** for processing sensitive personal information and privileged information; the University is fully committed to complying.

If consent is needed for any specific use not mentioned, it will be collected at the appropriate time. You may withdraw consent at any time (unless otherwise provided by law). The University **will not use personal data without prior consent for wholly automated decision-making** that affects you.

## Chapter IV: How We Share, Disclose, or Transfer Information

The University may share, disclose, or transfer personal data:

a. **Public posting** — acceptance, financial aid awards, merit scholarship grants, class lists, class schedules (online, school bulletin boards, campus locations)
b. **With parents/guardians/next of kin** — as required by law or on a need-to-know basis to protect health, safety, and security
c. **With potential donors, funders, benefactors** — for scholarship, grants, financial assistance
d. **Scholar's graduation brochures** — distributed to donors
e. **List of graduates and awardees** — distributed during commencement
f. **NPC and government agencies** — when required by law
g. **Accreditation and university ranking** entities
h. **Eligibility for sports/academic competitions**
i. **Court orders, subpoenas, legal obligations**
j. **Internal research/surveys** for institutional development
k. **Publicize achievements** — bulletin boards, website, social media, publications
l. **Journalistic content** — University publications, bulletin boards, website, social media
m. **Marketing/advertising** — photos, videos, brochures, website, newspaper ads, bulletin boards
n. **Live stream University events**

## Chapter V: How We Store and Retain Your Information

Data is stored in paper and electronic formats, including databases shared between University units. Access is **limited to authorized personnel** with legitimate interest in carrying out contractual duties.

Unless otherwise required by law or specified in applicable University policies, personal data may be **retained indefinitely for historical or statistical purposes**. Where a retention period is mandated, all affected records are properly disposed of after the specified period.

## Chapter VI: How You May Exercise Your Rights with Respect to Your Personal Data

You have rights specified in the DPA. If you exercise any of these rights, the University will respect your decision and handle it according to the law.

## Policy Revisions

Changes to this Policy may be made. Notification will be made through the website and other communication means when permissible. **Modifications are effective immediately upon posting.**

Other University policies consistent with this one continue to apply. If any provision is unenforceable or invalid, the invalidity does not affect the validity of other provisions, which remain in full force and effect.

## Consent

By signing the consent form, the student agrees:

- The collection and processing of personal data is governed by **Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012)** and other relevant Philippine laws.
- By registering as a student, they allow AdZU to collect, use, and process personal data where a **legitimate educational or institutional interest** exists.
- Information provided is **true and accurate**. AdZU reserves the right to revise decisions if information is found untrue or incorrect.
- Agreement to this Policy is a **condition of admission**.
- Issues will be **settled amicably** with AdZU before arbitration or court proceedings within Philippine jurisdiction.
